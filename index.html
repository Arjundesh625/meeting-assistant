Privacy & Limitations

Important considerations and workarounds for screen sharing

üö´

Screen Share Detection is IMPOSSIBLE

Chrome extensions cannot automatically detect when you're sharing your screen. This is a fundamental browser security restriction that cannot be bypassed.

You must use manual controls to hide the assistant when screen sharing.

Available Workarounds

‚úÖ Recommended Approach

Keyboard Shortcut + Second Monitor

  1. Set up a second monitor (or use a laptop screen + external monitor)
  2. Display the meeting on your main monitor (the one you share)
  3. Show the assistant answers on your second monitor
  4. Use Ctrl+Shift+H as a backup to quickly hide if needed

This provides the safest and most convenient setup for using the assistant during meetings.

Usage Guide

How to use the extension effectively during meetings

Getting Started

1

Start the Assistant

Click the extension icon and press "Start Assistant" before joining the meeting for best results.

2

Join Your Meeting

Join your video conference as normal. The extension will automatically start transcribing audio.

3

View AI Answers

Answers will appear in your chosen display mode (overlay, popup, or notifications).

4

Hide When Screen Sharing

Press Ctrl+Shift+H to instantly hide the overlay before you start screen sharing.

5

Stop the Assistant

When the meeting ends, click "Stop Assistant" to end transcription and API calls.

Tips & Best Practices

Troubleshooting

  • Check that you've granted microphone/tab audio permissions
  • Ensure the correct tab is selected for audio capture
  • Verify your meeting platform is producing audio
  • Try refreshing the page and restarting the extension
  • Switch to a higher-quality API (AssemblyAI recommended)
  • Ensure good audio quality in your meeting
  • Check your internet connection speed
  • Consider using a different microphone/audio source
  • Check your internet connection latency
  • Switch to faster APIs (Deepgram for speech, GPT-4 for AI)
  • Reduce the frequency of AI query triggers
  • Use "be concise" in your AI prompts
  • Ensure Developer Mode is enabled in chrome://extensions
  • Check that all files are in the correct folder structure
  • Look for errors in the extension details page
  • Verify manifest.json is valid JSON
